My name is Adam Cegiełka

With 3 Polish Championship titles and 3rd place in the team classification of the World and European Motoparagliding Championships I've experienced first-hand the critical importance of quality control in high-stakes situations. Translating this precision to the tech world, I’m actively developing as a manual and automation tester.

My goal? To ensure that software performs with the same unchangeable reliability as my airborne equipment. Driven by innovation, I'm eager to improve software quality.

In addition to my work in technology, I'm passionate about the Italian language, culture, history, and cuisine of this fascinating country. My interests allowed me to open myself to other cultures and communicate with people all over the world.
